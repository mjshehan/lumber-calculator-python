About

The Amazing Lumber Calculator exists to forever change the way we visit Home Depot.

I’ve done a few DIY carpentry projects – a fence, a deck, a planting box and a balance beam that was really just a board laying in the grass.

Each and every time two things happened:

  1. I figured out how much lumber I needed to buy.
  2. I was wrong.

I hated being wrong. It tore me up inside. I knew that there had to be a better way!

Thus, the Amazing Lumber Calculator was born.

It uses a linear solving algorithm to figure out what sizes of dimensional lumber you should buy, and how to cut them up to minimize waste on your project.

It will generate an image of the optimal cut diagram for you, and if you want, you can even save it for free!

One word of warning:

From experience, I can tell you that the Amazing Lumber Calculator cannot fix our cuts when we measure them wrong… My dad actually made up a saying: “measure twice, cut once.” We’d all do well to remember those wise words.

Also, watch your fingers around the saw.

-Mike
