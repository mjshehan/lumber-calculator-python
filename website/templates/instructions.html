Instructions

1. Enter the dimensions of the lumber you need to cut.

  1. Enter the available lengths of a particular width/thickness of dimensional lumber using whole numbers (no fractions or decimals) .
    Note, this is a linear calculator. You will need to run it once for each different width / thickness.
    For example if you need pieces with dimension 2x8, 2x4, 1x6, and 1x1 (of some lengths), run the calculator once for your 2x8 pieces, once for your 2x4's etc.

  2. Enter the lengths of each piece for that thickness, using whole numbers. Use commas or spaces to separate the values. For example, if you need 6 pieces of 2x4 lumber, 2 pieces at 94 inchesand 4 piece at 35 inches, you would enter <94 94 35 35 35 35> or <94, 94, 35, 35, 35>


  3. Submit -- depending on the length of your list of cut pieces, the program can take a few seconds or a few minutes to complete. Do not navigate away from the page

Limitations

  • Currently must run the calculator once for every diferent width of lumber you are using.
  • Currently do not have support for decimal / fraction inputs
  • Lists of extremely small values do not compute (for example, one cut of 2 inches form an 8ft board)
